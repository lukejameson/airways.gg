Add timezone drift monitoring that periodically checks localToUtc offset correctness and alerts via Telegram if the offset ever drifts.

💡 Why & How

The timezone fix replaced Luxon with native Intl, but there's no guard to detect if Intl's Europe/London resolution ever breaks again (e.g., after a Node.js upgrade, ICU data change, or container image change). Adding a periodic check in the guernsey scraper live loop with a Telegram alert will catch any future regression within minutes.

🎁 Expected Outcome

When the scraper is running during BST, it verifies every 30 minutes that localToUtc produces the correct UTC offset. If the offset is wrong (0 instead of 1 during BST, or vice versa in GMT), a Telegram alert fires with the detected vs expected offset values.
