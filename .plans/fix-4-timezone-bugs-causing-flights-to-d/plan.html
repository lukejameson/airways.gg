💡 Why & How

The codebase has a long history of toggling between timeZone: 'UTC' and timeZone: 'Europe/London' in display format functions, combined with a migration (0014) that converted BST wall-clock timestamps to true UTC. The scrapers now correctly store UTC, and the pg driver reads UTC — but the frontend is inconsistent: the FlightCard correctly uses Europe/London, while the flight detail page and stats page still use UTC or extract raw UTC hours. All fixes are surgical — mostly 1-3 line changes in specific files.

🎁 Expected Outcome

All times display correctly as Guernsey local (Europe/London) across the flight board, flight detail page, and stats page. Historical actual_departure/actual_arrival times are verified and fixed where needed. A shared format helper prevents future regressions.
